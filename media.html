Music and Media

Halo CE Legendary World Record Progression

Currently working on a massive research project on the world record progression for individual levels of Halo: Combat Evolved. Using data from past and archived databases that I have accessed through web history and other members of the community and compiling them all into separate documents to make a professionally made and styled video with voiceover.
Currently in progress. Images and video will be finished for the first episode around summer of 2021.



HaloRuns 2020 Grand Prix Compilation

This video project was a compilation of the HaloRuns 2020 Grand Prix, where people from around the world competed for the best times across all of the games of the Halo franchise. I both hosted and created the event for the community with thousands of members.
Video link is here.
Made using Sony Vegas Pro 17.0.
