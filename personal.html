Summer Games Done Quick 2019

In the summer of 2019, I participated in SGDQ 2019, the largest speedrunning event in the world broadcast live to millions of people around the world. This charity event raises millions of dollars for Doctors without Borders. I was one of the featured runners running The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim at the event to over 100,000 live viewers.
